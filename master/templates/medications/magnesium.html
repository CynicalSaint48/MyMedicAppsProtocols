Magnesium Sulfate

Indications:
Obstetric: Pre-eclampsia / Eclampsia; Pre-term labor
Cardiac: Refractory ventricular fibrillation / ventricular tachycardia; Torsades des points
Pulmonary: Refractory bronchospasm
Contraindications:
Renal Failure
Mechanism of action:
Bronchospasm: bronchial smooth muscle relaxation
Torsades: decreases influx of calcium suppressing early afterdepolarizations
Eclampsia: depresses the CNS producing anticonvulsant effects
How Supplied
1 gram per 2 ml vial = (500 mg per ml) 50%
