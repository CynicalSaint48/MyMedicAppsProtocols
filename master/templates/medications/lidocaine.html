Lidocaine

Indications:
Ventricular ectopy
Cardiac arrest - ventricular fibrillation; ventricular tachycardia
Analgesia related to IO line insertion
Contraindications:
Known hypersensitivity reaction
2nd degree type 2 & 3rd degree heart block
Mechanism of action:
Class 1B anti-arrhythmic
Blocks sodium channels
Decreases myocardial automaticity
Local anesthetic
Blocks sodium channels
How Supplied
100 mg in 5 ml = (20 mg per ml) prefilled syringe
