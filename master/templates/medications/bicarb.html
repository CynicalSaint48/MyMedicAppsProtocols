Sodium Bicarbonate

Indications:
Cardiac arrest with prolonged resuscitation time
Severe acidosis (pH < 7.0)
Significant hyperkalemia
Tricyclic antidepressant overdose with ECG changes
Significant crush injury
Contraindications:
Known hypersensitivity
Mechanism of action:
Plasma buffer (HCO3-)
Sodium electrolyte (Na+)

50 mEq in 50 ml = (1 mEq per ml)
