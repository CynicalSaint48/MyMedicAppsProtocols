Welcome to My Medic Apps; This application is meant to provide easy and quick access to your Pre-Hospital Protocols, Standing Orders, Medication Formulary and additional tools.

The web-based format allows for cross-platform access. All Windows, Mac, Android and IOS devices are supported. The responsive layout changes depending on the size and shape of the screen that it is being viewed on.

Users are able to Expand or Collapse sections of the Protocol, allowing them to configure a view that is relevant to their use-case.

Entry of Patient Age and Weight (or other patient variables) will integrate patient specific treatment directly into the protocol, including differing medications, dosages, maximums, or contraindications.

Together, these features provide you with a view of each of your protocols that is specific to the provider utilizing them, and the patient being treated.
